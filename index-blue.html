Our Story

More Than Recycling. It's Humanity.

TruHelpPC was built on a simple truth: technology is not a luxury — it's a lifeline. While millions of devices collect dust in American homes, children in conflict-torn communities around the world are being left behind. We bridge that gap. Every device you donate gets securely wiped, lovingly refurbished, and placed in the hands of someone who needs it most. Your old device could be the reason a child chooses education over despair.
