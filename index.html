The Founder

More Than Recycling. It's Personal.

TruHelp PC wasn't built in a boardroom. It was built by someone who lived the other side of this story.

Condi Mbemba came to America as a refugee with nothing but drive and a love for computers that started with a beat-up old laptop his mom gave him at 16. He worked nights cleaning stadiums. He taught himself everything. He built something.

TruHelp PC exists because Condi knows — from lived experience — what one device can do for a kid who has nothing. He's not guessing. He was that kid.
